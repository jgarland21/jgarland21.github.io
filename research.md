<h2>Current Work</h2>
<p>Most broadly, I am interested in observational astronomy, both for its own research products as well as its use as a test of computational and theoretical predictions. Recently, my focus has been on two projects attempting to better understand how galaxies evolve and what they can tell us about how the universe behaves. This work, done over the past two years with Professors Karen Masters and Daniel Grin, has led me to a particular interest in extragalactic astronomy, especially in the optical and radio regimes.
</p>
